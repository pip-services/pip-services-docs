### Description

The TokenType class defines different types (categories) of tokens, such as "number", "symbol" and "word".


### Enumeration members

The codes are as follows:

- **Unknown** = iota
- **Eof**
- **Eol**
- **Float**
- **Integer**
- **HexDecimal**
- **Number**
- **Symbol**
- **Quoted**
- **Word**
- **Keyword**
- **Whitespace**
- **Comment**
- **Special**
